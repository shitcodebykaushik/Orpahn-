About Us

We build lifelong support systems for orphaned children.

HopeCare partners with local leaders to provide safety, education, and well-being long after a crisis ends.

Our Story

From a single community home to a global network.

Founded in 2010, HopeCare started with one goal: no child should navigate life alone. Today, our teams support children across 15 countries with programs that keep them safe, educated, and emotionally supported.

We build lasting partnerships with schools, hospitals, and social workers so every child receives consistent care.

Our Values

We lead with dignity, accountability, and partnership.

Child-first care

Every decision is centered on safety, belonging, and the long-term needs of each child.

Community-led action

We build local capacity so communities can support children for generations.

Transparent impact

We track outcomes, publish results, and ensure every donation reaches a child in need.

Milestones

Key moments that shaped HopeCare.

2010

First community home opened

We launched our first safe house for children affected by conflict.

2015

Education scholarships scaled

Scholarships expanded across three countries with local school partners.

2020

Emergency response network

Rapid-response teams launched to support children in crisis zones.

2026

120 partner homes worldwide

HopeCare now supports children across 15 countries with long-term care.
